Mission: ASTRALIS 1-2

Embark on the intergalactic odyssey of Astralis 1-2, where we'll traverse cosmic realms, harness hyperdimensional propulsion and discover new heights of space exploration.

The ASTRALIS 1-2 Orbital Flyby mission will commence with the launch of a powerful Heavy rocket, carrying a suite of advanced imaging instruments and scientific sensors. Once in orbit around Earth, the spacecraft will perform a series of trajectory adjustments to synchronize its path. During the flyby, the spacecraft will come within a distance of approximately 10,000 kilometers from the planet, allowing for detailed observation and data collection. The high-resolution multi-spectral cameras will capture stunning imagery of the planet surface, while thermal imaging sensors will provide insights into temperature variations and potential volcanic activity. The LIDAR system will contribute to mapping the topography and atmospheric composition of the planet.
