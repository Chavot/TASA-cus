ABOUT

History

Throughout our illustrious history, TASA has been at the forefront of advancing Taiwan's space capabilities, making remarkable strides that continue to shape the future. With unwavering dedication, visionary leadership, and an unyielding commitment to excellence.

At TASA, we firmly believe that the sky is not the limit when it comes to Taiwan's aspirations in space, and we are proud to lead the way in realizing the limitless potential that awaits us among the stars.

Integrity

Integrity and trustworthiness form the bedrock of our organization. We take immense pride in upholding the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in everything we do. From the meticulous planning of our missions to the development of cutting-edge satellites.

Our actions reflect a deep sense of responsibility and a genuine concern for the welfare of the global space community.

Fulfillment

Customer satisfaction is of paramount importance to us, and it lies at the heart of everything we do. We prioritize delivering exceptional experiences and exceeding the expectations of our customers. From the moment you embark on a PINNACLE mission, we are dedicated to ensuring your satisfaction every step of the way.

Join us on a mission, and experience a level of customer satisfaction that goes above and beyond, as we make your dreams of space exploration a reality.
